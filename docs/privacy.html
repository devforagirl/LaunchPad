LaunchPad

Privacy Policy

Important: The developer has disclosed that this extension will not collect or use your data.

Data Usage Declaration

This developer declares that your data is:

  • Not being sold to third parties, outside of the approved use cases
  • Not being used or transferred for purposes that are unrelated to the item's core functionality
  • Not being used or transferred to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes

Information We Collect

LaunchPad does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The only data stored locally on your device is:

Locally Stored Data (on your device only)

  • Your alarm settings for launch notifications
  • This data never leaves your browser

Third-Party Services

LaunchPad uses the following third-party services:

  • RocketLaunch.live API: To fetch public rocket launch schedule data. No personal information is sent to this service.
  • Chrome Extension APIs: For notifications, storage, and alarms. These are standard browser APIs managed by Google.

Browser Permissions

LaunchPad requires the following permissions to function:

  • Notifications: To display launch alarm alerts
  • Storage: To save your alarm settings locally
  • Alarms: To schedule launch reminder notifications
  • Active Tab: For basic extension functionality

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
